Introduction

The Energy Club is a student-run club dedicated to exploring innovative solutions in the field of energy and sustainability. Our mission is to create a platform for students to learn, share knowledge, and develop practical skills in renewable energy, energy efficiency, and sustainable technologies

Vision

To bring together BIT students, scholars, alumni and professors of all levels irrespective of discipline, who so ever are interested in energy and sustainability issues To facilitate students in making a tangible impact on the BIT campus and ensure that it becomes greener, energy- efficient and more sustainable

Mission

To make people aware about the need, concept and methodology of energy conservation and environment protection and its benefits to the individual, society and nation as a whole by regularly organizing talks, seminars, workshops, exhibitions, write ups, quizzes etc. in inter and intra college levels
